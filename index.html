The Power to Heal

A visualization-based relaxation practice you can use to support comfort and calm. (This does not replace medical treatment.)

Practice: “Ocean Waves” Relief

  1. Focus: Bring attention to the area that feels uncomfortable.
  2. Breathe: Inhale slowly through the nose, exhale gently.
  3. Visualize: Imagine “ocean waves” of comfort moving into that area.
  4. Repeat: Keep sending waves until the area feels calmer or more numb.

Test & Body-Scan Variation

If you want a simple test, you can do a gentle version: make a fist with one hand and lightly tap the other hand (do not injure yourself). As soon as discomfort begins, try the “waves” visualization and see whether the sensation calms down.

Whole-body targeting (relaxation scan)
  • Bring attention to a region (arms, chest, legs) as if it’s the whole focus.
  • “Clench” your focus mentally (not physically) and direct comfort waves through the area.
  • Move slowly from one region to the next like a guided body scan.

Fountain of Youth (Myth & Inspiration)

Juan Ponce de León is often associated with the legend of the “Fountain of Youth,” the idea that drinking from it could restore youth. This story is commonly shared as a myth and inspiration for the human desire to stay young.

Practice: “Youthfulness Frequency” Focus

“Target the thought of youth and youthfulness with your mind, feel it as a frequency, then apply it to your face and body with focused attention and pressure.”

  1. Find the feeling: Think of “youth / youngness” and notice the sensation it creates.
  2. Face focus: Imagine a gentle layer across your face and soften your expression.
  3. Apply attention: Maintain steady focus (no strain) as if “merging” that feeling into your face.
  4. Relaxation pressure: If you use the idea of “Jhore,” keep it gentle—avoid pain or strain.
